The tope Tree advise from The book are.

  1. Develop a burning desire for your goal:

    Hill emphasizes the importance of having a strong desire for achieving your goals. He calls this a "burning desire" that motivates you to take action and persist in the face of obstacles. Without a clear and intense desire for success, you are unlikely to achieve it.

  2. Create a definite plan for achieving your goal:

    Once you have a clear desire, Hill recommends creating a specific plan for achieving your goal. This plan should be detailed, practical, and flexible enough to adjust as needed. By having a plan, you can stay focused and take consistent action towards your goal.

  3. Surround yourself with like-minded people

    Hill also stresses the importance of surrounding yourself with people who share your vision and support your goals. By associating with positive, motivated individuals, you can gain knowledge, support, and inspiration. Conversely, if you surround yourself with negative or apathetic people, you risk losing your motivation and enthusiasm for your goal.
